Title: Brother and former US President Gerald A. Ford has died
Words: 680

================================================================================

Tweet Follow @PietreStones Brother and former US President Gerald A. Ford has died GERALD RUDOLPH FORD 4-Jul-1913 26-Dec-2006 38th US President, 1974-77 December 26, 2006 Brother & former President Gerald A. Ford died today in California aged 93. The same day as Brother & former President Harry S. Truman died in 1972. Bro. Ford died at his home in Rancho Mirage, about 130 miles east of Los Angeles for natural causes. MASONIC RECORD Initiated: September 30, 1949, Malta Lodge No. 465, Grand Rapids, Michigan, along with his half-brothers Thomas Gardner Ford (1918-1995), Richard Addison Ford (1924-) and James Francis Ford (1927- ). The Fellowcraft and Master Mason Degrees were Conferred by Columbia Lodge No. 3, Washington, D.C., on April 20 and May 18, 1951, as a courtesy to Malta Lodge. Brother Ford was made a Sovereign Grand Inspector General, 33°, and Honorary Member, Supreme Council A.A.S.R. Northern Jurisdiction at the Academy of Music in Philadelphia, on September 26, 1962, for which he served as Exemplar (Representative) for his Class. Brother and President Ford was unanimously elected an Active Member of the International Supreme Council, Order of DeMolay and its Honorary Grand Master, at its Annual Session held at Orlando, Florida, April 6-9, 1975; Brother Ford held this post until January 1977, at which time he became a Past Honorary Grand Master, receiving his Collar and Jewel on October 24, 1978 in Topeka, Kansas, from the Hon. Thomas C. Raum, Jr., Grand Master, Order of DeMolay. GERALD RUDOLPH FORD 4-Jul-1913 26-Dec-2006 38th US President, 1974-77 December 26, 2006 Brother & former President Gerald A.
