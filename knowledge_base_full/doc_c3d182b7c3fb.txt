Q. Which of the following is the largest tributary of Indus River? [A] Chenab River[B] Satluj River[C] Ravi River[D] Jhelum River Answer: ANotes:Explanation: The Chenab is the largest tributary of the Indus. It is formed by two streams, the Chandra and the Bhaga, which join at Tandi near Keylong in Himachal Pradesh.Hence, it is also known as Chandrabhaga.
