Enter your email Address Enter your email Address Enter your email Address BOTN Story Battle of the Nations Battle of the Nations – is a world championship in historical medieval battle (HMB), full-contact martial arts in armour with steel weapons. It has unique concept and you will unlikely find anything remotely the same in the world. This is THE main event for HMB community. Every year fighters, supporters and their fans from all around the globe travel to one special place where “Battle of the Nations” is held in order to compete in duel and group categories for the World Champions’ titles The idea and concept of “Battle of the Nations” was born in 2009. That same year we held a summit of the future participating countries in Kharkiv, Ukraine. Four countries took part: Russia, Ukraine, Belarus and Poland. After a huge success of the first festival, the status of World Championship was recognized by the worldwide community and it was decided to hold the championship annually. It was held in Aueges-Mortes, France in May with a huge success.
