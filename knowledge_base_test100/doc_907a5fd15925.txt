Recovers most of the user's HP. The effect of moon herbs changes according to the condition they're in. Full Moon herbs are collections of wilted herbs that emit a faint light even in their wilted state. Weight: 0.1 Use Restores 600 Health Points Availability Location Cost Amount Comment Nexus 1000 unlimited Sold by Patches, the Hyena 1-2 1600 unlimited Sold by Dregling Merchant 1-3 1600 unlimited Sold by Dregling Merchant 3-1 1600 unlimited Sold by Once Royal Mistress 4-1 1000 unlimited Sold by Graverobber Blige 4-1 Shard of Mercurystone 10 Trade one Shard of Mercurystone with Sparkly the Crow to obtain ten Full Moon Grass. NB. A single instance of an item can be traded with the crow per playthrough 5-1 2000 unlimited Sold by Filthy Woman 5-2 2000 unlimited Sold by Filthy Woman all areas drop usually one usually dropped by stronger enemies (Red Eye Knight for instance) in early game cycles ie.
